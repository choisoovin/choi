"CAT KING," 2021

Medium: Piskel, Unity

Description:

I participate in making a game in the team and executed character design, and platform design, and did all of Unity programming. Cat King is about a young cat named Sable who has dreams to become the king of his domain. He knows the only way to be a King is to reach the top of his tower. This vertical 2d platformer game is Sable’s journey of climbing up his tower and claiming his throne.
